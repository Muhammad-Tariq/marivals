# Iron Man Complete Guide

Tony Stark's Iron Man suit combines devastating firepower with unmatched aerial mobility, making him one of the most versatile duelists in Marvel Rivals. This comprehensive guide covers everything from basic flight mechanics to advanced energy management techniques.

## Why Play Iron Man?

- **Aerial Dominance**: Control the high ground with superior flight capabilities
- **Consistent Damage**: Hitscan repulsors ensure reliable damage output
- **Versatility**: Effective at multiple ranges and situations
- **High Skill Ceiling**: Rewards mechanical skill and positioning mastery

## Abilities Breakdown

### Repulsor Blast (Primary Fire)
- **Damage**: 70 per shot
- **Headshot Multiplier**: 2x (140 damage)
- **Fire Rate**: 300 RPM
- **Range**: 30m optimal, 40m maximum
- **Type**: Hitscan

**Usage Tips**:
- Aim for headshots to maximize DPS
- Lead targets slightly when they're moving laterally
- Burst fire at long range for better accuracy
- Maintain optimal range (25-30m) for consistent damage

### Unibeam (Secondary Fire)
- **Damage**: 150 total (channeled)
- **Cooldown**: 12 seconds
- **Range**: 35m
- **Duration**: 2 seconds
- **Type**: Channeled beam

**Usage Tips**:
- Use against stationary or slow-moving targets
- Combine with Armor Overdrive for maximum damage
- Cancel early if target escapes to save cooldown
- Excellent for shield breaking

### Powered Flight (Mobility)
- **Fuel Duration**: 8 seconds
- **Recharge Time**: 4 seconds (when grounded)
- **Speed**: 15 m/s

**Usage Tips**:
- Manage fuel carefully - never fully deplete
- Use short bursts for positioning adjustments
- Land periodically to recharge faster
- Combine with natural cover for safety

### Armor Overdrive (Defensive)
- **Cooldown**: 15 seconds
- **Duration**: 4 seconds
- **Damage Reduction**: 40%
- **Bonus**: Increased movement speed

**Usage Tips**:
- Activate before engaging in 1v1s
- Use to escape dangerous situations
- Combine with Unibeam for aggressive plays
- Save for critical moments, not on cooldown

### Invincible Iron Man (Ultimate)
- **Charge Required**: 2000 points
- **Duration**: 8 seconds
- **Effects**: Unlimited flight fuel, increased fire rate, damage boost

**Usage Tips**:
- Use to secure team fights or contest objectives
- Activate from high ground for maximum impact
- Focus supports and low-health targets first
- Don't waste on single targets unless critical

## Advanced Techniques

### Energy Management
Master the art of balancing your abilities:
1. **Never Empty**: Keep 20% fuel reserve for escapes
2. **Cooldown Tracking**: Know when abilities return
3. **Burst Patterns**: Fire in controlled bursts to manage heat
4. **Resource Priority**: Flight > Armor > Unibeam

### Optimal Flight Patterns
- **Figure-8 Movement**: Unpredictable aerial patterns
- **Vertical Juke**: Sudden altitude changes to dodge
- **Strafe Hovering**: Side-to-side movement while firing
- **Dive Bombing**: Quick descents for surprise attacks

### Positioning Strategies
1. **High Ground Control**: Maintain aerial advantage
2. **Sightline Management**: Use buildings and structures
3. **Escape Routes**: Always know your exit path
4. **Team Proximity**: Stay within support range

### Combat Combos
- **Burst Combo**: Armor Overdrive → Unibeam → Repulsors
- **Poke Pattern**: Repulsor bursts → Reposition → Repeat
- **Dive Combo**: Descend → Unibeam → Armor Overdrive → Escape
- **Ultimate Combo**: Ult → Focus Supports → Reposition → Clean Up

## Map-Specific Strategies

### Hellfire Gala (Excellent)
- Utilize open spaces for flight advantage
- Control upper balconies and rooftops
- Watch for enclosed areas where flight is limited
- Coordinate with team for aerial bombardment

### Hydra Base (Good)
- Use corridors for Unibeam channeling
- Maintain high ground on objective points
- Be cautious of tight spaces limiting mobility
- Flank through outdoor areas

### Midtown (Excellent)
- Dominate skyscraper rooftops
- Use building sides for cover while flying
- Excellent sightlines for repulsor poke
- Vertical mobility advantage over most agents

## Team Synergies

### Best Teammates
- **Captain America**: Shield provides cover for positioning
- **Doctor Strange**: Portals extend your effective range
- **Mantis**: Healing keeps you in the fight longer
- **Thor**: Coordinate aerial attacks for devastating combos

### Team Compositions
- **Dive Comp**: Iron Man + Spider-Man + Mantis
- **Poke Comp**: Iron Man + Hawkeye + Luna Snow
- **Balanced**: Iron Man + Captain America + Doctor Strange

## Counters and How to Handle Them

### Hard Counters
**Hawkeye**
- Problem: One-shot potential from range
- Solution: Unpredictable flight patterns, use cover, pressure with team

**Magneto**
- Problem: Can pull you from the sky
- Solution: Bait abilities, maintain distance, focus him first

**Hela**
- Problem: High burst damage, area denial
- Solution: Dive from unexpected angles, use Armor Overdrive

### Skill Matchups
**Scarlet Witch**
- Stay mobile to avoid projectiles
- Pressure during ability cooldowns
- Use Armor Overdrive to tank damage

**Black Widow**
- Outrange her pistols
- Watch for grapple hooks
- Maintain aerial advantage

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Overextending**: Flying too deep without escape plan
2. **Fuel Mismanagement**: Running out of fuel in bad positions
3. **Tunnel Vision**: Focusing one target while ignoring threats
4. **Poor Ultimate Timing**: Wasting ult on won/lost fights
5. **Ignoring Supports**: Not prioritizing enemy healers

## Beginner Tips

- Start by mastering basic flight controls
- Practice aim in training mode
- Learn one map thoroughly before expanding
- Focus on staying alive over getting kills
- Communicate with your team

## Advanced Tips

- Track enemy ultimate status
- Pre-fire common positions
- Use audio cues for awareness
- Master quick 180° turns while flying
- Learn animation cancels

## Loadout Recommendations

### Aggressive Playstyle
- Focus on damage output perks

- Increased flight speed

### Defensive Playstyle
- Enhanced Armor Overdrive duration
- Faster fuel recharge

1. **Flight Course**: Navigate maps using only flight
2. **Aim Training**: Hit moving targets in practice range
3. **Energy Management**: Complete fights without running out of fuel
4. **1v1 Practice**: Duel against various agents

## Conclusion

Iron Man rewards players who master his complex kit with incredible carry potential. Focus on positioning, energy management, and consistent aim to dominate matches. Remember: you're not just a damage dealer - you're a mobile threat that forces enemies to constantly look up.

**Key Takeaways**:
- Master flight fuel management
- Maintain optimal engagement range
- Use Armor Overdrive proactively
- Prioritize high-value targets
- Never stop moving

Good luck, and may your repulsors always find their mark!
