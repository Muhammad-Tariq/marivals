# Thor Complete Guide

The God of Thunder brings overwhelming power and crowd control to Marvel Rivals. As a vanguard, Thor excels at initiating fights, disrupting enemy formations, and protecting his team while dealing massive damage.

## Why Play Thor?

- **High Damage Output**: Devastating melee and ranged attacks
- **Crowd Control**: Multiple abilities to disrupt enemies
- **Tankiness**: High health pool with self-sustain
- **Team Utility**: Provides space and protection for allies
- **Versatility**: Effective in both brawl and poke scenarios

## Abilities Breakdown

### Mjolnir Strike (Primary)
- **Damage**: 95 per hit (melee)
- **Attack Speed**: 250 RPM
- **Range**: 5m (melee), 35m (thrown)
- **Type**: Melee/Projectile hybrid

**Usage Tips**:
- Melee deals more damage than thrown
- Thrown Mjolnir returns automatically
- Can hit multiple enemies in a line when thrown
- Use melee in close range, throw for poke

### Mjolnir Throw (Alternate Fire)
- **Damage**: 95 base, 150 headshot
- **Cooldown**: Instant return
- **Range**: 35m
- **Type**: Projectile

**Usage Tips**:
- Aim for grouped enemies for multi-hits
- Throw while retreating to maintain pressure
- Recall instantly by pressing ability again
- Use to finish low-health targets at range

### Lightning Strike (Ability 1)
- **Damage**: 120 in center, 60 outer
- **Cooldown**: 10 seconds
- **Radius**: 8m
- **Type**: Area of Effect

**Usage Tips**:
- Combo with crowd control for guaranteed hits
- Use to clear objectives
- Excellent for grouped enemies
- Can hit enemies above and below

### Warrior's Fury (Ability 2)
- **Duration**: 5 seconds
- **Cooldown**: 15 seconds
- **Effects**: Increased damage, damage reduction
- **Bonus**: Movement speed increase

**Usage Tips**:
- Activate before engaging
- Use to survive burst damage
- Combine with ultimate for maximum impact
- Don't waste on poke damage

### Awakening Storm (Ultimate)
- **Damage**: 200+ over duration
- **Duration**: 6 seconds
- **Radius**: 15m
- **Effects**: Continuous lightning, crowd control

**Usage Tips**:
- Use on objectives or grouped enemies
- Combine with Warrior's Fury for survivability
- Forces enemies to scatter or die
- Excellent for team fight initiation

## Advanced Techniques

### Mjolnir Management
- **Throw Timing**: Throw during enemy abilities
- **Recall Tricks**: Recall mid-flight for mind games
- **Angle Shots**: Use geometry for multi-target hits
- **Melee Priority**: Always melee when in range

### Positioning Strategies
1. **Front Line Presence**: Lead your team's advance
2. **Objective Control**: Stand on points to contest
3. **Peel for Supports**: Protect your healers
4. **Space Creation**: Force enemies to respect your zone

### Combat Patterns
- **Engage**: Warrior's Fury → Lightning Strike → Melee
- **Poke**: Mjolnir Throw → Reposition → Repeat
- **All-In**: Ultimate → Warrior's Fury → Melee Focus
- **Peel**: Lightning Strike → Melee → Throw

## Map-Specific Strategies

### Royal Palace (Excellent)
- Control throne room with lightning strikes
- Use pillars for cover between engagements
- Dominate narrow corridors with melee
- Ultimate is devastating in enclosed spaces

### Yggdrasill Path (Excellent)
- Throw Mjolnir along bridges for multi-hits
- Lightning strike on narrow pathways
- Use tree roots for cover
- Excellent for controlling choke points

### Arakko (Good)
- Open spaces favor Mjolnir throws
- Use rock formations for cover
- Lightning strike on objectives
- Be cautious of long sightlines

## Team Synergies

### Best Teammates
- **Loki**: Illusions create chaos for your engagement
- **Hela**: Combined area damage is overwhelming
- **Mantis**: Healing keeps you in the fight
- **Captain America**: Double tank front line

### Effective Compositions
- **Brawl Comp**: Thor + Hulk + Mantis
- **Asgardian Synergy**: Thor + Loki + Hela
- **Balanced**: Thor + Iron Man + Doctor Strange

## Counters and Solutions

### Hard Counters
**Black Panther**
- Problem: High mobility, can kite you
- Solution: Save Lightning Strike for his engage, stick with team

**Scarlet Witch**
- Problem: High burst damage from range
- Solution: Use Warrior's Fury, close distance quickly, throw Mjolnir

**Hawkeye**
- Problem: Long-range damage
- Solution: Use cover, throw Mjolnir for pressure, engage with team

### Skill Matchups
**Hulk**
- Trade melee damage
- Use Lightning Strike when he engages
- Kite with Mjolnir throws

**Magneto**
- Bait his abilities
- Engage after he uses crowd control
- Focus him in team fights

## Common Mistakes

1. **Overextending**: Going too deep without team
2. **Wasting Ultimate**: Using ult on single targets
3. **Ignoring Supports**: Not protecting your healers
4. **Poor Fury Usage**: Activating too early or late
5. **Throw Spam**: Throwing when melee is better

## Beginner Tips

- Focus on staying alive first
- Practice Mjolnir throw accuracy
- Learn when to engage vs. poke
- Communicate with your team
- Use Lightning Strike on cooldown

## Advanced Tips

- Track enemy escape abilities
- Pre-throw Mjolnir to common positions
- Use ultimate to zone, not just damage
- Master quick melee-to-throw transitions
- Learn optimal Warrior's Fury timing
